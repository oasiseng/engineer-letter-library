# Legal and Professional Boundaries

## Non-Advice Constraint
This repository provides drafting structure and process guidance only. It does not provide engineering advice.

## No Delegation of Licensure
Nothing in this repository supersedes the duty, judgment, or signature authority of a licensed Professional Engineer.

## No Implied Certification
Template language must avoid implying compliance certification unless explicitly approved and issued by the responsible PE.

## Jurisdictional Variability
Code references, terminology, and acceptance criteria vary by jurisdiction. All local determinations require qualified professional review.

## Liability-Conscious Drafting
Contributors should prefer clear scope statements, explicit assumptions, and explicit limitations.
