Per the request of the Owner, we have reviewed the engineering plans for the construction of a new metal building at [PROJECT_ADDRESS] [ZIP_CODE].

I hereby affirm that existing subsurface groundwater conditions and soils data were examined, and that the foundation design submitted has accounted for these conditions.
