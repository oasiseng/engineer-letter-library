Scope and Basis of Review
The subject roof was re-roofed circa 2021, and in-progress inspections were not performed at the time of installation. As such, this certification is issued after-the-fact and is limited to verifying code compliance of the roofing system as installed, strictly for permitting purposes.
This determination is based upon the following:
A final non-invasive visual inspection of the roof covering and appurtenances
Review of available construction documentation and photographs
Limited invasive verification, performed in the presence of the licensed roofing contractor, consisting of the temporary removal of a shingle course to confirm:
Roof underlayment type and installation
Shingle fastening pattern and attachment
Following verification, the affected area was restored using matching shingles, re-nailed in accordance with manufacturer requirements, and sealed with roofing cement.
