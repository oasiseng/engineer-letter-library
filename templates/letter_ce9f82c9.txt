Nevertheless, appropriate best management practices (BMPs) are included to ensure compliance with the Town’s erosion and sediment control expectations for sub-acre projects.

Drainage Pattern & Impact
The proposed grading has been designed to maintain these natural drainage patterns, preventing the creation of any new concentrated discharge points or diversion of runoff onto adjacent parcels. Finished grading around the new slab ensures a minimum 2% slope away from the structure for the first 10 feet in all directions, consistent with good engineering practice and the International Residential Code (IRC R401.3) drainage requirements.
No alterations to offsite drainage patterns are proposed, and the improvements have been designed to prevent ponding or diversion of runoff onto adjacent properties. As such, no adverse offsite drainage impacts are anticipated.
