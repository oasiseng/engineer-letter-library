Drainage Pattern & Impact
Stormwater from the site will continue to drain naturally toward the southeast and south, following existing topography and discharging into an adjacent drainage easement located along the rear of the property. The proposed grading maintains natural flow paths and does not introduce any new concentrated discharge points. No alterations to offsite drainage patterns are proposed, and the improvements have been designed to prevent ponding or diversion of runoff onto adjacent properties. As such, no adverse offsite drainage impacts are anticipated.
